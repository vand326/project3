Censorship in American Version of Sailor Moon
1. Sailor Uranus and Sailor Neptune were originally girlfriends.
2. Few episodes were not translated due to being filler episodes, some being inappropriate for viewers.
3. Some characters had their genders changed.
4.The final season of Sailor Moon, Sailor Moon Stars, was never released in America.
5.Blood, violence, and death were censored due to being inappropriate for viewers.
